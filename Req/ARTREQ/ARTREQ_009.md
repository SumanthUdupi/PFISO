# Art Asset Requirement: ARTREQ_009

## 1. Asset Title
**Potted Monstera Deliciosa**

## 2. Asset Type
Environment / Prop (Nature)

## 3. Detailed Description
A lively, split-leaf philodendron (Monstera) in a ceramic pot. This adds organic shape and the "Forest Green" accent color to the scene, vital for the "Cozy" aesthetic.

## 4. Style Guidelines
- **Aesthetic:** Vibrant, Healthy, Organic.
- **Leaves:** Large, glossy, with characteristic holes (fenestrations).
- **Pot:** Geometric or simple cylinder.

## 5. Technical Specifications
- **Dimensions:** 1.0m tall.
- **File Format:** FBX
- **Polycount:** ~1500 tris.
- **Textures:** Albedo, Normal, Transmission/Subsurface (for leaves).
- **Color Palette:**
  - Leaves: #3a5a40 (Deep Forest Green) -> #ccd5ae (New growth gradient)
  - Pot: #eaddcf (Sand/Terra Cotta)

## 6. Lighting & Color Theme
- **Translucency:** Leaves should allow light to pass through slightly (backlighting effect) creating a vibrant green glow.
- **Shadows:** Casts complex, interesting shadows on walls/floor.

## 7. Visual References
- Real Monstera plants.
- "House Flipper" plants.

## 8. Integration Notes
- Place in corners or near the window.
- Leaves should not clip through walls; model with a "front" side.

## 9. Portfolio Impact
Demonstrates organic modeling, foliage texturing, and understanding of translucency/subsurface scattering shaders.
