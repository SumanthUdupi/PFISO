# Art Asset Requirement: ARTREQ_005

## 1. Asset Title
**Ergonomic Office Chair - Fabric**

## 2. Asset Type
Environment / Prop

## 3. Detailed Description
A high-quality office chair that looks comfortable for long coding sessions. Instead of a "gamer racer" chair, this is a "Herman Miller-esque" aesthetic mesh chair with grey/sage fabric, emphasizing the cozy/professional vibe.

## 4. Style Guidelines
- **Aesthetic:** Modern Ergonomic.
- **Tone:** Comfort first.
- **Materials:** Mesh back, fabric cushion, hard plastic base.

## 5. Technical Specifications
- **Dimensions:** Standard office chair size.
- **File Format:** FBX
- **Polycount:** 1500-2000 tris (curved surfaces).
- **Color Palette:**
  - Fabric: #ccd5ae (Muted Sage)
  - Frame: #2d2424 (Dark Grey/Black)
  - Base: Chrome or Matte Black.

## 6. Lighting & Color Theme
- **Subsurface Scattering:** (Optional) If possible, mesh back should let some light through.
- **Sheen:** Fabric should have a high roughness (cloth shader look).

## 7. Visual References
- Herman Miller Aeron (simplified).
- IKEA Markus chair.

## 8. Integration Notes
- Pivot point at the floor/wheels center for rotation.
- Can be placed slightly crooked to look "lived in".

## 9. Portfolio Impact
Demonstrates modeling complex curved surfaces and multiple material definitions (metal, plastic, fabric) on a single asset.
