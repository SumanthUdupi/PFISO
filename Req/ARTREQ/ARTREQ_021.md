# Art Asset Requirement: ARTREQ_021

## 1. Asset Title
**Ceramic Coffee Mug**

## 2. Asset Type
Prop / Interactive

## 3. Detailed Description
A handcrafted-style ceramic mug filled with coffee. It sits on the desk. Crucial for the "cozy" vibe. It should have a slightly imperfect, handmade geometry.

## 4. Style Guidelines
- **Aesthetic:** Artisanal / Pottery.
- **Glaze:** Speckled or two-tone.

## 5. Technical Specifications
- **Dimensions:** Standard mug size.
- **File Format:** FBX
- **Polycount:** ~300 tris.
- **Color Palette:**
  - Glaze: #fefae0 (Cream) with #d4a373 (rim).
  - Coffee: #2d2424 (Dark brown).

## 6. Lighting & Color Theme
- **Roughness:** Glaze should be glossy (low roughness), coffee liquid very glossy.
- **Steam:** Will attach a particle emitter for steam later.

## 7. Visual References
- Heath Ceramics mugs.

## 8. Integration Notes
- Interactive: Player can "sip" (click interaction).
- Needs a distinct collision box for clicking.

## 9. Portfolio Impact
Small detail that invites interaction.
