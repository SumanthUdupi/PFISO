# Art Asset Requirement: ARTREQ_020

## 1. Asset Title
**Round Side Table**

## 2. Asset Type
Environment / Prop

## 3. Detailed Description
A small, round metal side table. Good for holding a coffee cup next to the reading chair (if one exists) or in a corner.

## 4. Style Guidelines
- **Aesthetic:** Industrial / Wireframe.
- **Material:** Powder coated metal.

## 5. Technical Specifications
- **Dimensions:** 0.4m diameter, 0.5m height.
- **File Format:** FBX
- **Polycount:** ~300 tris.
- **Color Palette:**
  - #ccd5ae (Sage) or #2d2424 (Black).

## 6. Lighting & Color Theme
- **Shadows:** Wireframe base casts intricate shadows.

## 7. Visual References
- West Elm metal side tables.

## 8. Integration Notes
- Floor placement.

## 9. Portfolio Impact
Prop variety.
