# Art Asset Requirement: ARTREQ_010

## 1. Asset Title
**Floating Wall Shelf (Set of 3)**

## 2. Asset Type
Environment / Decor

## 3. Detailed Description
Simple wooden shelves that mount directly to the wall with hidden brackets ("floating"). They provide vertical space for displaying awards, small plants, or collectibles.

## 4. Style Guidelines
- **Aesthetic:** Minimalist.
- **Material:** Light wood (matching floor or desk).
- **Shape:** Simple plank.

## 5. Technical Specifications
- **Dimensions:** Various lengths (0.6m, 0.8m, 1.0m).
- **File Format:** FBX
- **Polycount:** < 50 tris per shelf.
- **Color Palette:**
  - Wood: #d4a373 (Warm Wood)

## 6. Lighting & Color Theme
- **Shadows:** Casting soft shadows down the wall is key to grounding them in 3D space.
- **Highlight:** Top surface needs to receive light to illuminate objects placed on it.

## 7. Visual References
- IKEA LACK shelves.
- Pinterest home office wall decor.

## 8. Integration Notes
- Must have snap points or collision for placing "chachkies" (items).
- Good vertical filler for empty wall space.

## 9. Portfolio Impact
Simple asset, but crucial for set dressing and vertical composition.
