# Art Asset Requirement: ARTREQ_002

## 1. Asset Title
**Standard Office Wall Section - Cream Plaster**

## 2. Asset Type
Environment / Structural

## 3. Detailed Description
A standard modular wall section. The texture should appear to be soft plaster or matte paint in a warm cream color. It serves as the primary backdrop for the scene. The top should have a simple crown molding and the bottom a humble baseboard to add detail without noise.

## 4. Style Guidelines
- **Aesthetic:** Clean, minimalist, warm.
- **Tone:** Calm and neutral.
- **Texture:** Very subtle grain, avoiding "perfectly flat" digital look.

## 5. Technical Specifications
- **Dimensions:** 4m (width) x 3m (height) x 0.2m (depth)
- **File Format:** FBX
- **Polycount:** Low (< 200 tris)
- **Textures:** Albedo, Normal (for plaster grain), Roughness. 1024x1024.
- **Color Palette:**
  - Wall: #fffcf5 (Warm Cream)
  - Trim: #fefae0 (Lighter Cream)
  - Shadow/AO: #ccd5ae (Muted Sage tint in shadows)

## 6. Lighting & Color Theme
- **Interaction:** Great canvas for projected shadows from windows/blinds.
- **Bounce Light:** High bounce factor to spread warm light through the room.

## 7. Visual References
- Minimalist Japanese office interiors.
- "Unpacking" game environments.

## 8. Integration Notes
- Modular snap points at corners.
- Double-sided geometry not required if exterior is never seen (portfolio piece typically interior only).

## 9. Portfolio Impact
Shows mastery of modular environment design and subtle texturing that supports lighting.
