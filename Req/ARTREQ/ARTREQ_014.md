# Art Asset Requirement: ARTREQ_014

## 1. Asset Title
**Modern Wood Ceiling Fan**

## 2. Asset Type
Environment / Prop

## 3. Detailed Description
A three-blade ceiling fan with solid wood blades. It rotates slowly, adding kinetic energy to the otherwise static room. It hangs from the center, potentially combined with the light fixture or separate.

## 4. Style Guidelines
- **Aesthetic:** Sleek, Modern, Tropical.
- **Speed:** Slow, lazy rotation.

## 5. Technical Specifications
- **Dimensions:** 1.2m diameter.
- **File Format:** FBX
- **Polycount:** ~800 tris.
- **Color Palette:**
  - Blades: #8c6a4a (Dark Walnut)
  - Motor Housing: Matte Black.

## 6. Lighting & Color Theme
- **Motion:** Moving shadows on the floor/walls create a dynamic "alive" feeling.

## 7. Visual References
- Haiku Home fans.
- Modern tropical interiors.

## 8. Integration Notes
- Pivot must be perfectly centered for rotation animation.
- Mounted on ceiling center.

## 9. Portfolio Impact
Adds life to the environment through subtle background animation.
