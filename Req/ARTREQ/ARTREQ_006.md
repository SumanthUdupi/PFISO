# Art Asset Requirement: ARTREQ_006

## 1. Asset Title
**Modular Open Bookshelf**

## 2. Asset Type
Environment / Prop

## 3. Detailed Description
A tall, open-back bookshelf that acts as a room divider or wall liner. Being open-back allows the wall color to show through, keeping the room feeling airy. It is filled with books, but not cluttered.

## 4. Style Guidelines
- **Aesthetic:** Industrial Soft.
- **Materials:** Metal frame with wood shelves.
- **Look:** Organized but active.

## 5. Technical Specifications
- **Dimensions:** 1.0m (width) x 2.2m (height) x 0.4m (depth)
- **File Format:** FBX
- **Polycount:** ~600 tris (without books).
- **Color Palette:**
  - Frame: #4a403a (Dark Iron)
  - Shelves: #d4a373 (Oak)

## 6. Lighting & Color Theme
- **Self-Shadowing:** Shelves must cast proper ambient occlusion shadows on the contents.
- **Silhouette:** Thin frame lines create nice visual rhythms against the wall.

## 7. Visual References
- Industrial pipe shelves.
- West Elm industrial shelving.

## 8. Integration Notes
- Should be populated with a separate "Book Set" prop or include pre-baked books for optimization.
- Make sure it doesn't z-fight with the wall.

## 9. Portfolio Impact
Demonstrates creation of "container" assets that frame other objects.
