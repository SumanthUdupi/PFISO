# Art Asset Requirement: ARTREQ_015

## 1. Asset Title
**Mid-Century Plant Stand**

## 2. Asset Type
Environment / Prop

## 3. Detailed Description
A small wooden stand to elevate a potted plant. Typically 3 legs, simple design. It adds verticality to the plant placement.

## 4. Style Guidelines
- **Aesthetic:** Mid-Century.
- **Material:** Teak or Oak.

## 5. Technical Specifications
- **Dimensions:** 0.4m height.
- **File Format:** FBX
- **Polycount:** ~200 tris.
- **Color Palette:**
  - Wood: #d4a373.

## 6. Lighting & Color Theme
- **Shadows:** Complex leg shadows on the floor.

## 7. Visual References
- West Elm plant stands.

## 8. Integration Notes
- Physics collider needed if player can knock it over (unlikely, but good practice).
- Holds ARTREQ_009 (Monstera) or smaller plants.

## 9. Portfolio Impact
Simple prop, helps grouping.
