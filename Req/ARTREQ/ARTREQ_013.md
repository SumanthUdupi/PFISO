# Art Asset Requirement: ARTREQ_013

## 1. Asset Title
**Classic Architect Desk Lamp**

## 2. Asset Type
Environment / Prop / Lighting

## 3. Detailed Description
A classic, articulated desk lamp (Pixar style) that sits on the desk. It provides task lighting for the workspace area.

## 4. Style Guidelines
- **Aesthetic:** Retro / Industrial.
- **Material:** Glossy Metal.
- **Color:** A strong accent color to break up the neutrals.

## 5. Technical Specifications
- **Dimensions:** 0.6m height (adjustable).
- **File Format:** FBX
- **Polycount:** ~1200 tris.
- **Color Palette:**
  - Metal: #da4b4b (Muted Red) or #3a5a40 (Forest Green) - **CHOICE: Forest Green** to match palette.
  - Springs: Silver/Chrome.

## 6. Lighting & Color Theme
- **Cone Light:** Source of the warm desk pool of light.
- **Reflection:** Glossy paint reflects the environment window.

## 7. Visual References
- Anglepoise Type 75.
- Pixar Luxo Jr.

## 8. Integration Notes
- Base sits on desk.
- Head aims at the laptop/notebook area.
- Emissive bulb surface.

## 9. Portfolio Impact
Classic prop that shows mechanical rigging potential and material variety.
