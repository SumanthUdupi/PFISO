# Art Asset Requirement: ARTREQ_037

## 1. Asset Title
**Ambient Room Fill Light**

## 2. Asset Type
Lighting

## 3. Detailed Description
The base ambient light for the room, preventing pitch black shadows. Represents indirect light bounce.

## 4. Style Guidelines
- **Type:** Area Light (Roof) or Hemisphere Light.
- **Mood:** Soft, indistinct.

## 5. Technical Specifications
- **Intensity:** Fill level (20%).
- **Color Palette:**
  - Sky Color: #d4a373.
  - Ground Color: #ccd5ae.

## 6. Lighting & Color Theme
- **Shadows:** Very soft or no shadows cast.

## 7. Visual References
- Global Illumination simulation.

## 8. Integration Notes
- Always on, dimmer in "Night Mode".

## 9. Portfolio Impact
Understanding of lighting ratios (Key vs Fill).
