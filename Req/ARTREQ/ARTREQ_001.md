# Art Asset Requirement: ARTREQ_001

## 1. Asset Title
**Modular Office Floor Tile - Warm Oak**

## 2. Asset Type
Environment / Structural

## 3. Detailed Description
A seamless, tiling floor asset representing warm, honey-colored oak wood flooring. This serves as the foundation of the cozy office space. It needs to feel grounded but not dark, reflecting the "Cozy Refined" palette. The wood grain should be visible but soft, avoiding high-contrast noise that would distract from gameplay elements.

## 4. Style Guidelines
- **Aesthetic:** Warm, inviting, clean but lived-in.
- **Tone:** Professional yet homey.
- **Reference Style:** Stylized realism or soft low-poly.
- **Key Feel:** "Warmth underfoot."

## 5. Technical Specifications
- **Dimensions:** 4m x 4m (tiling)
- **File Format:** FBX (Mesh), PNG (Textures)
- **Polycount:** Low (< 50 tris) - essentially a plane.
- **Textures:** Albedo, Normal, Roughness. 1024x1024.
- **Color Palette:**
  - Base: #d4a373 (Warm Wood)
  - Highlight: #eaddcf (Soft Cashmere)
  - Shadow: #8c6a4a (Darker Wood)
- **Roughness:** 0.6 (Satin finish, not glossy).

## 6. Lighting & Color Theme
- **Interaction:** Should catch warm sunlight beams (`#d4a373`) to create cozy hotspots.
- **Ambient Occlusion:** Subtle baking at edges where walls meet.
- **Color Temperature:** 3500K (Warm White) reflection.

## 7. Visual References
- Modern home office setups on Pinterest.
- Animal Crossing: New Horizons (Wooden flooring textures).
- Firewatch (Tower interiors).

## 8. Integration Notes
- Must tile perfectly on the grid.
- Will support placing rugs and furniture on top without Z-fighting (ensure pivot is at 0,0,0 bottom).

## 9. Portfolio Impact
Demonstrates ability to create fundamental tiling environmental assets that set the mood without dominating the visual hierarchy.
