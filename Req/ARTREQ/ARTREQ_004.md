# Art Asset Requirement: ARTREQ_004

## 1. Asset Title
**Minimalist Executive Desk - Walnut**

## 2. Asset Type
Environment / Prop (Hero Object)

## 3. Detailed Description
The central piece of the office. A sturdy, clean-lined desk with a beautiful wood grain. It has tapered legs and a slight mid-century modern influence. It serves as the player's main interaction zone.

## 4. Style Guidelines
- **Aesthetic:** Mid-century Modern.
- **Tone:** Professional, expensive but simple.
- **Wear:** pristine but matte finish.

## 5. Technical Specifications
- **Dimensions:** 1.8m x 0.8m x 0.75m
- **File Format:** FBX
- **Polycount:** ~800 tris (bevels on edges for catching light).
- **Textures:** Albedo, Normal, Roughness, AO map.
- **Color Palette:**
  - Wood: #4a403a (Deep Espresso/Walnut)
  - Accents (Feet): #d4a373 (Brass/Gold)

## 6. Lighting & Color Theme
- **Reflectivity:** Satin wood finish should show subtle reflections of the window.
- **Contrast:** Dark wood provides necessary contrast against the light floor and walls (#fffcf5).

## 7. Visual References
- West Elm "Mid-Century Desk".
- Firewatch desk assets.

## 8. Integration Notes
- Must have collision mesh for placing laptop, coffee, etc.
- Legs should not clip through the rug.

## 9. Portfolio Impact
Showcases hero prop modeling, UV unwrapping for wood grain continuity, and material definition.
