# Art Asset Requirement: ARTREQ_035

## 1. Asset Title
**Monitor Screen Glow**

## 2. Asset Type
Lighting

## 3. Detailed Description
A RectAreaLight positioned just in front of the laptop screen. It simulates the screen casting light onto the user (invisible) and the desk mat.

## 4. Style Guidelines
- **Type:** Cool / contrast light.
- **Color:** bluish white.

## 5. Technical Specifications
- **Intensity:** Low/Subtle.
- **Color Palette:**
  - #e0f7fa (Cool White).

## 6. Lighting & Color Theme
- **Complementary:** Provides a cool blue accent to balance the overwhelming warm orange/yellow of the room.

## 7. Visual References
- Cinematic shots of people working at computers at night.

## 8. Integration Notes
- Linked to laptop "open" state.

## 9. Portfolio Impact
Subtle lighting storytelling.
