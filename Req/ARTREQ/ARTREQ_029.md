# Art Asset Requirement: ARTREQ_029

## 1. Asset Title
**Wire Mesh Trash Can**

## 2. Asset Type
Prop

## 3. Detailed Description
A classic office wastebasket. Contains crinkled paper balls (ideas that didn't make the cut).

## 4. Style Guidelines
- **Aesthetic:** utilitarian.
- **Material:** Black wire mesh.

## 5. Technical Specifications

- **File Format:** FBX
- **Polycount:** ~200 tris (using alpha cutout for mesh) OR ~1000 tris (real geometry). **Recommendation:** Alpha texture.
- **Color Palette:**
  - #4a403a (Dark Iron).

## 6. Lighting & Color Theme
- **Transparency:** See-through mesh adds depth.

## 7. Visual References
- Standard office bin.

## 8. Integration Notes
- Under desk.
- Paper balls as separate meshes inside.

## 9. Portfolio Impact
Optimization (alpha test usage).
