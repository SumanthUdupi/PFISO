# Art Asset Requirement: ARTREQ_007

## 1. Asset Title
**Geometric Area Rug - Aztec/Boho**

## 2. Asset Type
Environment / Decor

## 3. Detailed Description
A large rectangular rug to define the desk area or reading nook. It features a simple geometric or boho pattern. The texture should look soft and fibrous, breaking up the tiling wood floor.

## 4. Style Guidelines
- **Aesthetic:** Boho Chic / Cozy.
- **Pattern:** Large simple diamonds or chevrons.
- **Texture:** Plush, slight displacement if possible.

## 5. Technical Specifications
- **Dimensions:** 2.5m x 3.5m
- **File Format:** FBX (Plane with thickness)
- **Polycount:** ~100 tris.
- **Textures:** Albedo, Normal (fabric weave), Roughness.
- **Color Palette:**
  - Base: #fffcf5 (Cream)
  - Pattern: #3a5a40 (Forest Green) and #d4a373 (Caramel)

## 6. Lighting & Color Theme
- **Softness:** Using a low sheen/high roughness value to make it look non-reflective and absorptive of light.
- **Grounding:** Helps darker furniture feel grounded (dark on light rug).

## 7. Visual References
- Ruggable "Boho" styles.
- Pinterest "Cozy Office Rugs".

## 8. Integration Notes
- Slight vertical offset (y=0.01) to avoid z-fighting with floor.
- Needs rounded corners geometry or alpha readout in texture.

## 9. Portfolio Impact
Shows ability to use texturing to convey softness and tactile feel in a hard-surface 3D environment.
