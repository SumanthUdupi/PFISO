# Art Asset Requirement: ARTREQ_031

## 1. Asset Title
**Felt Desk Mat**

## 2. Asset Type
Prop

## 3. Detailed Description
A large, soft felt mat that sits under the keyboard and mouse. It visually groups the input devices and adds material contrast to the hard wood desk.

## 4. Style Guidelines
- **Aesthetic:** Minimalist Workspace.
- **Material:** Heather Grey Wool Felt.

## 5. Technical Specifications
- **Dimensions:** 0.8m x 0.4m.
- **File Format:** FBX
- **Polycount:** ~50 tris.
- **Color Palette:**
  - #4a403a (Dark Grey) or #d4a373 (Brown Leather variation).

## 6. Lighting & Color Theme
- **Fuzz:** Needs a "fuzz" shader or detail normal map to look soft.

## 7. Visual References
- Grovemade felt mats.

## 8. Integration Notes
- Z-bias requirement to sit just above table surface.

## 9. Portfolio Impact
Material definition (Felt).
