# Art Asset Requirement: ARTREQ_039

## 1. Asset Title
**Global Volumetric Fog**

## 2. Asset Type
Atmosphere

## 3. Detailed Description
A very subtle uniform fog that lifts the blacks and softens distant objects (if the room is large enough) or just softens the lighting contrast.

## 4. Style Guidelines
- **Density:** Very Low.
- **Color:** Warm cream.

## 5. Technical Specifications
- **Color Palette:**
  - #fffcf5.
- **Density:** 0.005.

## 6. Lighting & Color Theme
- **Softness:** Removes the harsh "CG clear air" look.

## 7. Visual References
- Unity HDRP Volumetrics (subtle settings).

## 8. Integration Notes
- Performance tuning required for mobile.

## 9. Portfolio Impact
Atmospheric composition.
