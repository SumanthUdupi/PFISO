# Art Asset Requirement: ARTREQ_038

## 1. Asset Title
**Floating Dust Motes (VFX)**

## 2. Asset Type
VFX / Atmosphere

## 3. Detailed Description
Slowly drifting particles clearly visible in the sunbeams (ARTREQ_033). They give the air "texture" and stillness.

## 4. Style Guidelines
- **Motion:** Brownian motion, very slow.
- **Size:** Tiny.

## 5. Technical Specifications
- **System:** Particle System.
- **Count:** ~50-100 active.
- **Color:** White (Alpha 0.5).

## 6. Lighting & Color Theme
- **Interaction:** Should only be visible when backlit by the window light.

## 7. Visual References
- The Last of Us (dust in beams).

## 8. Integration Notes
- Constraint to room bounds.

## 9. Portfolio Impact
Subtle polish that breaks "static" game feel.
