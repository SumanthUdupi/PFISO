# Art Asset Requirement: ARTREQ_018

## 1. Asset Title
**Low Office Cabinet / Credenza**

## 2. Asset Type
Environment / Prop

## 3. Detailed Description
A low storage unit with sliding doors. It sits against the wall and provides a surface for the printer or plants.

## 4. Style Guidelines
- **Aesthetic:** Scandi / Modern.
- **Material:** Painted white wood with oak top.

## 5. Technical Specifications
- **Dimensions:** 1.2m x 0.5m x 0.6m.
- **File Format:** FBX
- **Polycount:** ~400 tris.
- **Color Palette:**
  - Body: #ffffff (White)
  - Top: #d4a373 (Oak)

## 6. Lighting & Color Theme
- **Cleanliness:** Bright white surface bounces light well.

## 7. Visual References
- IKEA Besta units.

## 8. Integration Notes
- Floor placement.
- Top surface is a key area for "clutter" props.

## 9. Portfolio Impact
Functional furniture design.
