# Art Asset Requirement: ARTREQ_008

## 1. Asset Title
**Modern Pendant Ceiling Light**

## 2. Asset Type
Environment / Lighting Fixture

## 3. Detailed Description
A simple, hanging pendant light with a matte finish dome shade. It hangs above the desk or center of the room. It is the source of artificial "fill" light in the evening scene.

## 4. Style Guidelines
- **Aesthetic:** Modern / Scandi.
- **Shape:** Smooth semi-sphere or cone.
- **Finish:** Matte ceramic or powder-coated metal.

## 5. Technical Specifications
- **Dimensions:** 0.4m diameter.
- **File Format:** FBX
- **Polycount:** ~300 tris.
- **Color Palette:**
  - Exterior: #ccd5ae (Sage) or #4a403a (Espresso)
  - Interior: #ffffff (White Reflective)
  - Bulb: Emissive Material.

## 6. Lighting & Color Theme
- **Emissive:** The bulb/interior faces must have an emissive texture to simulate being "on".
- **Light Source:** A point light will be parented to this asset in the engine.

## 7. Visual References
- Muuto pendant lamps.
- Sims 4 modern lighting.

## 8. Integration Notes
- Pivot at the top of the cord for easy ceiling placement.
- Cord length should be adjustable or have variants.

## 9. Portfolio Impact
Demonstrates creation of functional lighting assets and utilization of emissive maps.
