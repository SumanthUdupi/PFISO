# Art Asset Requirement: ARTREQ_012

## 1. Asset Title
**Wooden Venetian Blinds**

## 2. Asset Type
Environment / Prop

## 3. Detailed Description
Wooden slat blinds to cover the large bay window. They modulate the incoming light, creating beautiful stripy shadows (gobos) on the desk and floor.

## 4. Style Guidelines
- **Aesthetic:** Warm, Organic.
- **Material:** Light timer.
- **Function:** Partially open.

## 5. Technical Specifications
- **Dimensions:** Fits Window (ARTREQ_003).
- **File Format:** FBX
- **Polycount:** ~400 tris (simplified slats).
- **Color Palette:**
  - Wood: #d4a373 (Warm Wood)
  - Strings: #fffcf5 (Cream)

## 6. Lighting & Color Theme
- **Shadows:** THIS IS CRITICAL. The blinds exist primarily to cast interesting shadow patterns ("venetian blind effect") on the room's interior.
- **Subsurface:** Thin slats might have slight light transmission.

## 7. Visual References
- Film noir lighting (softened for cozy theme).
- Standard wooden blinds.

## 8. Integration Notes
- Placed inside the window frame.
- Needs to be separate from the window for potential "open/close" animation later.

## 9. Portfolio Impact
Demonstrates understanding of how geometry affects lighting and mood.
