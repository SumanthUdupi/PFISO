# Art Asset Requirement: ARTREQ_040

## 1. Asset Title
**Window Bloom Profile**

## 2. Asset Type
Post-Processing

## 3. Detailed Description
Bloom settings specifically tuned to make the window area "blow out" slightly, creating that dreamy high-exposure look for the outdoors.

## 4. Style Guidelines
- **Threshold:** High (only hits skybox and sun).
- **Intensity:** Moderate spread.

## 5. Technical Specifications
- **Threshold:** 0.9.
- **Radius:** Large.
- **Intensity:** 1.5.

## 6. Lighting & Color Theme
- **Dreamy:** Helps mask low-res skyboxes and focuses attention indoors.

## 7. Visual References
- Photography "dreamy" filters.

## 8. Integration Notes
- Post-process volume.

## 9. Portfolio Impact
Post-processing stack mastery.
