# Art Asset Requirement: ARTREQ_019

## 1. Asset Title
**Minimalist Wall Clock**

## 2. Asset Type
Environment / Prop

## 3. Detailed Description
A simple, round analog wall clock. No numbers, just ticks. It suggests the passage of time in a relaxed way.

## 4. Style Guidelines
- **Aesthetic:** Modern.
- **Face:** White or Sage Green.
- **Hands:** Wood or Black.

## 5. Technical Specifications
- **Dimensions:** 0.3m diameter.
- **File Format:** FBX
- **Polycount:** ~200 tris.
- **Color Palette:**
  - Rim: #d4a373 wood.
  - Face: #fffcf5.

## 6. Lighting & Color Theme
- **Subtle:** Just a background detail.

## 7. Visual References
- Muji wall clocks.

## 8. Integration Notes
- Wall mounted.
- Hands can be animated to show real time if desired.

## 9. Portfolio Impact
Time-keeping prop.
