# Art Asset Requirement: ARTREQ_044

## 1. Asset Title
**Sunset HDRI Skybox**

## 2. Asset Type
Environment / Lighting

## 3. Detailed Description
A high-dynamic range image of a sunset. It provides the lighting information for the reflections on the glossy desk and laptop screen. Visible through the window.

## 4. Style Guidelines
- **Colors:** Pink, Orange, Purple gradient.
- **Clouds:** Soft, fluffy.

## 5. Technical Specifications
- **Resolution:** 4k.
- **Format:** .EXR or .HDR.
- **Projection:** Equirectangular.

## 6. Lighting & Color Theme
- **Reflection:** Creates beautiful pink/purple highlights on metallic objects.

## 7. Visual References
- Poly Haven "Sunset" HDRIs.

## 8. Integration Notes
- Environment Lighting source.

## 9. Portfolio Impact
Image-based lighting (IBL) usage.
