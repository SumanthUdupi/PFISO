# Art Asset Requirement: ARTREQ_042

## 1. Asset Title
**Warm Cinematic LUT**

## 2. Asset Type
Post-Processing / Color

## 3. Detailed Description
A Look Up Table (LUT) texture that grades the final render. It boosts warm tones (oranges/yellows) and slightly desaturates blues, giving the "Kodak Portra" or "Indie Game" look.

## 4. Style Guidelines
- **Aesthetic:** Analog Photography.
- **Vibe:** Nostalgic.

## 5. Technical Specifications
- **Format:** .CUBE or .PNG (32x32x32).
- **Contrast:** Mild curve.
- **Black Point:** Slightly lifted (faded blacks).

## 6. Lighting & Color Theme
- **Uniformity:** Binds all assets together into a cohesive palette.

## 7. Visual References
- VSCO filters (A6/M5).

## 8. Integration Notes
- Post-process stack.

## 9. Portfolio Impact
Color grading artistry.
