# 07 - Minigames & Easter Eggs

**Project:** Interactive 3D Portfolio (PFISO)

## Overview
Small pockets of gameplay to break up the reading experience and showcase technical prowess.

## 1. The "Terminal" Hack
**Location:** The main computer.
- **Trigger:** Typing `hack` or interacting with a specific minimized window.
- **Gameplay:** A falling-text matrix style mini-game or a simple typing speed test (Typeracer clone).
- **Reward:** Unlocks "Senior Developer" badge on the resume.

## 2. Physics Toys
**Location:** Everywhere.
- **Basketball Hoop:**
    - Small hoop on the back of the door.
    - Interact with a foam ball to "Hold".
    - Charge throw with Left Mouse Button (Hold for power). 
    - **Scoreboard:** Digital counter increments on basket.
- **Newtonâ€™s Cradle:**
    - Real-time physics chain reaction.
- **Jenga / Block Tower:**
    - Stack of blocks that can be knocked over.

## 3. The "Retro" Console
**Location:** Lounge Area (TV).
- **Interaction:** Turn on console.
- **Game:** "Snake" or "Pong" played purely on the in-game TV screen using arrow keys.
- **Immersive:** The player controls the game *while* standing in the office (Render-to-Texture), not a full-screen takeover.

## 4. Hidden Collectibles
**Hunt:** "Find the 5 Golden Floppy Disks".
- **Locations:** Under the couch, behind the plant, on top of the bookshelf, inside a drawer.
- **Reward:** Unlocks the "Secret Room" (see Game Design doc).

## 5. The "Secret Room" (Developer Museum)
**Access:** Unlocked via collectibles or Konami Code.
**Content:**
- "Concept Art" gallery of this portfolio.
- "About the Author" (Personal hobbies, travel photos).
- "Thank You" wall with names of inspirations.
